About Us

We started with a simple observation — and turned it into a mission to transform how India handles electronic waste.

Our Origin

Pavitra Foundation began with the e-Pavitra initiative — a hands-on effort to collect and responsibly dispose of electronic waste at the community level. As the initiative grew, so did our understanding of the problem's sheer scale.

We saw committed volunteers, dedicated collectors, and willing recyclers — but no system connecting them efficiently. Data was lost, devices were misrouted, and valuable materials were wasted. That's when we knew: we needed technology.

🌍 Our Vision

A world where no electronic device ends its lifecycle in a landfill. Where every device is tracked, recovered, and re-entered into the circular economy.

🎯 Our Mission

To develop grassroots-level circular economy logistics software that empowers local communities, collectors, and recyclers to work as one seamless system.

💚 Our Values

Transparency, sustainability, and community-first thinking. We believe the best technology is invisible — it just makes people's work easier and the planet healthier.

🔬 Our Approach

Data-driven, grassroots-grounded. We work directly with informal waste collectors and certified recyclers to build systems that work in the real world, not just on paper.

Why Technology?

Manual tracking systems break down at scale. A single collector may handle dozens of devices a day across multiple localities. Without a digital backbone, it's impossible to optimise routes, ensure proper disposal, or generate the lifecycle data needed for policy change.

Our circular economy software bridges that gap — lightweight enough for field workers, powerful enough for impact reporting.
