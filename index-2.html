What We Do

Three Pillars of Change

🗺️

Track

We map the entire lifecycle of electronic devices — from manufacture to disposal — using our circular economy logistics software.

⚙️

Optimise

Our systems identify inefficiencies in e-waste collection chains and suggest smarter, greener routing for recyclers and collectors.

🤝

Connect

We link grassroots waste collectors with certified recyclers, bridging the gap between informal and formal e-waste economies.
