Our Solution

Circular economy logistics software — built for the grassroots, designed for scale.

How It Works

Our platform creates a connected digital layer over the existing e-waste collection ecosystem. Every device, every collector, every recycler gets a node in the network — and data flows between them in real time.

  • 1

    Device Registration

    When a device is collected, it's logged in our system with a unique ID, device type, condition, and location. Even basic feature phones can scan a QR code to register a pickup.

  • 2

    Life Cycle Tracking

    The device is tracked at every handoff — collector to aggregator, aggregator to recycler. Every movement is timestamped and geo-tagged, creating a complete audit trail.

  • 3

    Route Optimisation

    Our logistics engine analyses collection density maps and suggests optimal pickup routes for collectors, reducing travel time and fuel use.

  • 4

    Recycler Matching

    Devices are automatically matched to certified recyclers based on device type, location proximity, and recycler capacity — ensuring the right device goes to the right facility.

  • 5

    Impact Reporting

    Collectors, NGOs, and municipalities get real-time dashboards showing kg collected, CO₂ saved, materials recovered — the data needed to scale impact and attract funding.

Built for India

Our system is designed from the ground up for low-bandwidth environments, multilingual users, and the realities of informal sector work. Offline-first functionality, simple interfaces, and WhatsApp integration make adoption frictionless even in rural areas.

Open & Collaborative

We believe in open data standards for e-waste. Our platform integrates with existing municipal systems, EPR portals, and third-party recycler databases — because a fragmented ecosystem is part of the problem, not the solution.
